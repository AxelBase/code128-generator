Creating GS1-128 (EAN-128) Shipping Labels in 2025

Published: November 29, 2025

GS1-128 is the global standard for logistics barcodes. This free generator creates fully compliant labels — no ZPL, no Bartender, no subscription required.

Format: ~FNC1 + Application Identifiers

Every GS1-128 barcode starts with FNC1, followed by data in parentheses:

  • ~FNC1(01)00012345678909(17)301225(10)BATCH123

Most Common AIs (Copy-Paste Ready)

  • SSCC-18: ~FNC1(00)001234567890123456
  • GTIN + Exp + Batch: ~FNC1(01)01234567890128(17)301225(10)ABC123
  • Serial + Lot: ~FNC1(01)01234567890128(21)SER123(10)LOT789

Compliance Checklist

  • FNC1 as first character
  • Correct AI parentheses
  • Valid check digit (automatically calculated)
  • Minimum X-dimension: 0.25 mm
  • Human-readable text below barcode
